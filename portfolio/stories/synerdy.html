Synerdy App

A Geekverse Hub

This story talks about the Synerdy App, an Android-based E-Commerce Application that can be used by buyers and sellers to facilitate their nerdy shopping experience. Synerdy App is the first comic book app in South Africa and the first comic book marketplace app in the world. You can purchase products and navigate your way around the app easily with our sleek and easy to understand User Interface.

Synerdy App is the most convinient platform you can use when it comes to selling or purchasing collectibles. There is an extensive list of categories to pick from making your shopping experience easier.

Using this platform gives you an advantage when it comes to your overall shopping experience. Not only can you sign up and purchase products but you can register and apply for a seller's account. That gives you the platform to sell as many products as you like. There is no limit to the number of products that you can put up for sale.

Features include a wishlist, cart, profile page, unlimited categories, unlimited products listings, contact us page, about us page and much much more, download the Synerdy App. on Google Play Store.

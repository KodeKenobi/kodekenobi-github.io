I AM AN

ENTREPRENEUR

I AM AN ENTREPRENEUR is here to help entrepreneurs build and grow their businesses. We do that through our summits, which up-skill entrepreneurs with key business skills. We also develop and manage impactful Enterprise and Suppliers Development (ESD) Programmes for multi-nationals and South African corporates in compliance with B-BBEE regulations.

Hosted by Matsi Modise, Vice-Chairperson of SiMODiSA and Founding CEO of Furaha Afrika Holdings, the first I AM AN ENTREPRENEUR Summit, in association with FNB, was amazing! It featured guest entrepreneur Vuyisile Zondi, the Founder of Corium Skincare, a proudly South African natural skincare range, who shared her journey and lessons on reinvention.
